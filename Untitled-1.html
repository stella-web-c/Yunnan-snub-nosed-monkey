Rhinopithecus bieti

Life habit

colony

The population of Rhinopithecus yunnanensis is small, mostly 20-60, and no large group of more than 150 has been found. It is a mixed population with many males and many females, and has social hierarchical behavior. A family usually consists of one male, 2-3 females and several young. There is no obvious seasonal vertical migration. The range of activity is different from the size of the monkey group, about 20-133.4 square meters, and the average density is 1.12-2.5 square meters

Favorite food for Yunnan snub nosed monkeys

They mainly eat the tender leaves and overwintering flower buds and leaf buds of Usnea and birch. They also eat the tender buds and young leaves of plants of Usnea and birch. They also eat bamboo shoots and young leaves of Fargesia in July and August. They also eat the fruit of lacquer tree in winter

They are lovely. Do you know where to see them?

It is distributed in Yunling mountains of Hengduan Mountain System on the southern margin of Himalayas, a small area between Lancang River and Jinsha River. Covering an area of about 20000 square kilometers, it extends northward to the tranquil mountains in Tibet, including Deqin County, Weixi County, Lijiang County, Jianchuan County, Lanping County, Yunlong County in Yunnan, and Mangkang County in Tibet
